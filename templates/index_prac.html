ABOUT AGRIGUARD

Agriguard is an advanced crop disease detection platform designed to help farmers and researchers identify and manage plant diseases efficiently. Our system utilizes AI-powered analysis to detect diseases like Early Blight, Late Blight, and Bacterial Blight in tomato plants.

Diseases We Detect


Early Blight

Identified by brown spots with concentric rings, this disease weakens plants by causing premature leaf drop, reducing fruit production.

Late Blight

A fast-spreading fungal disease that creates dark, water-soaked lesions on leaves. It thrives in wet conditions and can quickly destroy entire crops if left untreated.

Bacterial Spot

Causes small, dark, water-soaked spots that lead to yellowing and eventual leaf drop, making plants more vulnerable to environmental stress.
